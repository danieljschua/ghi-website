What do we do?

Global Health Initiative is a TJHSST Club dedicated to raising awareness and taking action on some of the largest health issues plaguing the world. With issues ranging from the water crisis, famine, drug abuse, the refugee crisis, and widespread disease, we hope to do all we can to alleviate these global situations. Even through small steps, we can make a big difference.
The club will have a new issue to tackle every school quarter, with presentations, guest speakers, and a special event to bring the TJ Community together in fighting these major problems.
